    Message from the District President

    Let us rise “From basics towards pinnacle”

    My dear Leos, I’d like to leave you with these words of advice. Be supportive, be genuine, be hard working and give everything your best from the bottom of your heart and never expect anything in return. It is my belief that we should be living with a pure heart by sharing with and caring about each other, to look after our community. Therefore, do keep that in mind and provide your best contributions to the movement and to the society in the upcoming years as well.
    Wish you all a wonderful Leoistic Year ahead!
    Be Bright, Be Brave & Be beautiful!.
