    Leo Mandakini Kariyawasam

    Leo District 306 C1

    Let us rise “From basics towards pinnacle”

    My dear Leos, I’d like to leave you with these words of advice. Be supportive, be genuine, be hard working and give everything your best from the bottom of your heart and never expect anything in return. It is my belief that we should be living with a pure heart by sharing with and caring about each other, to look after our community. Therefore, do keep that in mind and provide your best contributions to the movement and to the society in the upcoming years as well.
    Wish you all a wonderful Leoistic Year ahead!
    Be Bright, Be Brave & Be beautiful!.

    Biography

    Born in 1994, Mandakini is the eldest Child in the family of 4 children and the only daughter of Mr. & Mrs. Kariyawasam. She started her education at Musaeus College, Colombo 07 where her personality was well groomed under the guidance of her teachers and mentors and roots of a remarkable journey were planted. Not limiting her endless capabilities to academics she excelled herself in extracurricular activities as well. She was engaged in Athletics and Netball during her School tenure. She was also a member of the dancing Troup in South Asian Games held in Colombo Sri Lanka 2006.
    After leaving her school education, Leo Mandakini initiated a conquering chapter in her life. She started a new path as a LEO and she together with PDP Leo Lion Shathika Karunaratne was instrumental in extending a new Leo Club back in 2014, well it is none other than the Leo Club of Battaramulla Synergy. She was introduced to the movement by PDP Leo Lion Shathika Karunaratne and at the same year she was appointed as the Charter President of Leo Club of Battaramulla Synergy and the President in the following Leoistic year as well. She is a risk taker and a challenging personality who took up herself into a greater height by accepting the challenges given to her in the charter year itself. She was indeed successful and she conquered. She lead the infant Leo club to become the Best New Leo Club in Sri Lanka back in 2014/15 which is once in a lifetime award for a Leo Club and she was awarded the most outstanding New Leo of Sri Lanka another Once in a Life time award for a Leo. Seeing her potential in the following year she was then appointed to the district council as an activity director for International Relations.
    She was one young lady who never failed to fulfil the entrusted responsibilities towards her and has always given her best. She has initiated some of the signature projects which were recognized in the Leo Multiple District Level and Sath Piyum which became the Most Outstanding Continuous project in 2017/18 in Multiple District 306 is an ideal example for that. She also initiated projects such as “Nirogi Piyasatahan” and “Ice Breaker” which became some of the signature projects in the District Level as well.
    Leo Mandakini continued to showcase her brilliant competencies in every angle possible after getting appointed to the District council for the past consecutive years. She has held the positions of
     Director International Relations (2015/2016)

Her capacity of fulfilling the responsibilities assigned to her above the expectations lifted her up to the Multiple Level. She was first invited to the Multiple District Council by the Past International Advisory Panelist PMDP Lion Rajitha Abeygunasekara and then again she was appointed to the Multiple District Council in the Leoistic years of 2017/18 & 2019/20

That enduring journey she began to walk in 2014 as a Synergy Leo was filled with everyday challenges where she committed herself to defeat the challenges to conquer each moment which lead to Leadership, Experience and Opportunity. Leo Mandakini, an inspiring leader, a pioneering hard worker, a friendly team player, and above all truly a LEO is willing to deliver her caliber for the betterment of the community, and for those in need sharing through our courage and dedication. She is a Bachelor of Business and holds a Double Major in Management and International Business from Edith Cowen University Australia. She is also holding an advanced diploma in French language (l’alliance francaise) and an advanced diploma in management accounting (CIMA,UK)
    She worked as an intern in hemas holdings plc in the department of group sustainability and corporate communication where she got the privilege to contribute her fair share in national level mass projects such as AYATI and Piyawara. Currently she works as a Senior Operations Analyst at Synergen Health LLC. Her father is a Businessman and a proprietor while her mother is a housewife. She has 3 younger brothers schooled at D.S.Senanayake College Colombo 7 and 2 out of her 3 brothers are Leos of the Leo club of Battaramulla Synergy and youngest brother is a Leo in D.S.Senanayake College. Her eldest sibling is a final year student in university of moratuwa faculty of engineering, 2 nd brother is an army cadet officer aligned to Sri Lanka Army and following up a bachelor’s degree in Logistics Management at Kotalawala Defence University and her youngest brother will be sitting for his A/L examination in coming September.
    Dear Leo, Lions and my dear friends I present you the Iron Lady of 306C1 District President for the Leoistic year 2021/22 Leo Mandakini Kariyawasam .
